## Column guidance

- **Issue Description** — the potential legal exposure in plain language, tied to a provided fact or an explicitly flagged assumption. Use `[CONFIRM: ...]` for open questions.
- **Practice Area** — the practice area(s) the issue belongs to; one row per issue, not per practice area.
- **Severity** — High / Medium / Low / Unknown, assigned in Workflow step 12.
- **Recommended Owner / Skill** — the practice-area specialist, sibling skill, or external counsel the issue routes to (feeds the Routing Map in Output Format section 3).
- **Blocking?** — whether the issue must be resolved before launch (feeds the draft go / hold / conditions recommendation). Yes / No / TBD.
- **Status** — current disposition of the issue.
