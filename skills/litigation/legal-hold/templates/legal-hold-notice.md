## What This Means in Practice

### You Must

- **DO** preserve all potentially relevant material in the categories and systems listed above, effective immediately.
- **DO** suspend any automated deletion, archiving, or overwriting that would affect covered material — contact your IT department or records team if you need assistance disabling such processes.
- **DO** notify counsel immediately if you discover that any responsive material has been deleted, lost, overwritten, or damaged — whether before or after receipt of this notice.
- **DO** contact counsel with any questions about the scope of this hold before assuming that any material is outside its coverage.
- **DO** acknowledge receipt of this notice as instructed in the Acknowledgment section below.
- **DO** forward this notice to any colleague or report who you believe may also possess responsive material, and notify counsel of their identity.

### You Must Not

- **DO NOT** delete, destroy, overwrite, or alter any document, communication, or data that may be covered by this hold, regardless of any routine retention or deletion policy or schedule.
- **DO NOT** route communications covered by this hold through a platform or method not listed above in an attempt to place them outside the scope of this notice.
- **DO NOT** discuss the existence or contents of this notice with anyone outside the company or organization other than counsel — preserve the confidentiality of this notice.
- **DO NOT** forward or distribute this notice to opposing parties, outside advisors, or any person not authorized by counsel to receive it.
- **DO NOT** assume that material on a personal device or personal account is outside the scope of this hold if it relates to work matters covered by the hold. Contact counsel if you are unsure.
- **DO NOT** wait to comply. Preservation obligations are immediate.
