## Column guidance

- **Requirement** — the discrete requirement as stated in the source; quote operative language where precision matters. Do not paraphrase in a way that narrows or expands scope.
- **Source ref** — section, provision, or paragraph reference in the requirement source. If verified via an eCFR connector, record the eCFR URL and snapshot date alongside.
- **Current control** — the control, policy, procedure, or system the organization described as addressing the requirement, or "No control described."
- **Status** — Met / Partial / Gap / Unknown, as defined in the skill's Workflow step 4. "Met" means the described control appears to address the requirement based on the information provided — not a legal compliance conclusion.
- **Severity** — High / Medium / Low for Partial and Gap items; "—" otherwise. Preliminary workflow assessment subject to attorney and management review.
- **Remediation (draft)** — plain-language type of action (e.g., draft a written policy, implement a system control, conduct training). Not a prescription of specific legal steps.
- **Owner (proposed)** — likely organizational function (legal, compliance, IT, HR, operations, finance); use `[OWNER: CONFIRM]` where unclear.
- **Attorney note** — `[CONFIRM: ...]` items, applicability questions, or interpretation issues requiring attorney judgment.
