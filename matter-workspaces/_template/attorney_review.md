# Attorney Review

> The supervising attorney gate for the whole matter. Draft work product for
> attorney review. No output in this workspace is relied upon, sent, filed, or
> signed until the supervising attorney has completed this gate. The agent never
> checks these boxes — the reviewing attorney does. See
> `skills/legal-methodology/attorney-review-gate/SKILL.md`.

## Matter-Level Sign-Off Checklist

- [ ] Parties, client role, and counterparty (if any) confirmed.
- [ ] Jurisdiction, governing law, and posture confirmed or flagged unknown.
- [ ] Every deadline in `matter_profile.md` independently verified — none computed.
- [ ] `facts.md` reviewed; established facts are sourced and disputed facts marked.
- [ ] `open_questions.md` reviewed; blocking questions resolved or owned.
- [ ] `source_log.md` reviewed; every relied-upon source classified.
- [ ] `citation_map.md` reviewed; every authority verified or flagged for verification.
- [ ] `unsupported_claims.md` has no `Open` entries.
- [ ] `assumptions.md` reviewed; no assumption silently treated as fact.
- [ ] Recommended quality checks in `quality_checks/` run and issues resolved.
- [ ] Each output in `outputs/` reviewed and marked Adopted or Superseded in `skills_used.md`.
- [ ] Privilege and confidentiality preserved; distribution limited per `matter_profile.md`.
- [ ] All `[CONFIRM: ...]`, `[VERIFY: ...]`, and `[ACTION: ...]` placeholders resolved.
