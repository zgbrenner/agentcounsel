# Unsupported Claims

> Draft work product for attorney review. Every statement that lacks adequate
> source support is logged here until it is resolved — supported by a verified
> source, rewritten as a flagged assumption, or removed. A visible unsupported
> claim is safe; a hidden one is not. This register feeds the source-validation
> and hallucination-red-team quality checks.

## Register

## Classification Vocabulary

Statements in this register typically carry one of:

- `unsupported` — no source supports the statement.
- `source-mentioned but insufficient` — a source touches it but does not support it.
- `contradicted by source` — a source cuts against the statement.
- `legal authority required` — needs a verified citation not yet provided.
- `attorney judgment required` — only the attorney can resolve it.
- `model-suggested authority requiring verification` — relies on an unverified
  model-surfaced authority.
- `unsupported or unverifiable authority` — relies on an authority that could not
  be located or verified.

## Resolution Rule

No draft leaves the matter as relied-upon work product while any entry here is
`Open`. The supervising attorney confirms each resolution in `attorney_review.md`.
