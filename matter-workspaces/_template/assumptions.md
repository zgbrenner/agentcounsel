## Classification Vocabulary

Assumptions are typically labeled with one of:

- `attorney judgment required` — the assumption stands in for a determination only
  the attorney can make.
- `legal authority required` — the assumption stands in for a legal proposition
  needing a verified citation.
- `source-mentioned but insufficient` — a source hints at the point but does not
  establish it, so the analysis assumes it.
- `unsupported` — assumed with no source; highest-risk, confirm first.
- `user-provided authority` — assumed on the user's representation (still verify).

## Audit Rule

Before any draft is relied upon, the supervising attorney reviews every
assumption here, confirms or corrects it, and confirms that no assumption has been
silently treated as an established fact in any output.
