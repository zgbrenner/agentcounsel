# Tax Matter Packs

## General Tax Issue Intake Matter Pack
- **When to use:** Early-stage tax issue intake and organization.
- **Required starting materials:** Jurisdiction, entity/taxpayer type, tax period, source docs, review purpose.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** `tax-issue-intake` → `tax-document-organizer` → `tax-provision-review-checklist` (if contracts).
- **Expected outputs:** Intake summary, document inventory, provision issue list.
- **Qualified tax professional checkpoints:** Gate confirmation, issue theme review, reliance approval.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** No tax computation, no filing, no final tax conclusions.
- **Handoff notes:** Intake gates and open questions flow into organizer and provision review.
