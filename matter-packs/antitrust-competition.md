# Antitrust / Competition Matter Packs

All packs produce draft work product for attorney review only.

## 1. Competitor Collaboration Matter Pack
- **When to use:** competitor collaborations, JVs, benchmarking, standard-setting, or competitor communications.
- **Required starting materials:** parties/roles, market context, collaboration docs, information-sharing protocols, and timeline.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** antitrust-risk-intake -> competitor-collaboration-review -> information-sharing-clean-team-review -> antitrust-compliance-policy-review.
- **Expected outputs:** intake summary, collaboration risk matrix, information-sharing controls gaps, policy updates.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before any competitor communication or data exchange.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no final legality conclusions or approvals to proceed.
- **Handoff notes:** intake facts feed collaboration review; collaboration outputs define clean-team controls; control gaps feed policy updates.

## 2. Pricing / Algorithmic Pricing Matter Pack
- **When to use:** pricing tools, dynamic pricing, AI/revenue management, common vendor concerns.
- **Required starting materials:** tool architecture, inputs/outputs, vendor materials, pricing governance docs, communication logs.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** antitrust-risk-intake -> pricing-algorithm-risk-triage -> information-sharing-clean-team-review -> antitrust-compliance-policy-review.
- **Expected outputs:** risk triage matrix, vendor diligence list, clean-team/control gaps, policy/training updates.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before deployment, pricing changes, or competitor-adjacent data use.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no approval of pricing conduct.
- **Handoff notes:** intake frames conduct; pricing triage identifies data/control concerns; those concerns drive clean-team and policy updates.

## 3. M&A Antitrust Matter Pack
- **When to use:** M&A, minority investments, roll-ups, JVs with antitrust/gun-jumping risk.
- **Required starting materials:** transaction summary, overlap facts, integration plan, diligence room, governance rights docs.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** merger-antitrust-issue-spotter -> information-sharing-clean-team-review -> gun-jumping-clean-team-checklist -> antitrust-compliance-policy-review.
- **Expected outputs:** issue list, filing/fact questions, clean-team inventory, pre-closing guardrails, policy gaps.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before filing decisions, pre-closing conduct, closing, or integration.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no reportability or clearance conclusions.
- **Handoff notes:** issue spotting drives sharing controls; sharing controls and deal covenants drive gun-jumping checklist; resulting gaps feed policy updates.

## 4. Distribution / Channel Strategy Matter Pack
- **When to use:** distribution agreements, reseller terms, channel restrictions, MFN/exclusivity/pricing clauses.
- **Required starting materials:** agreement drafts, channel map, pricing terms, counterparties, market facts.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** distribution-restraints-review -> exclusivity-mfn-pricing-review -> antitrust-risk-intake.
- **Expected outputs:** restraints table, clause risk matrix, negotiation issues, consolidated intake and escalation list.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before executing terms or communicating restrictions.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no enforceability or legality conclusions.
- **Handoff notes:** distribution review identifies restrictive terms; clause review deepens economic/operational implications; intake consolidates unresolved risks and document requests.

## 5. Trade Association Matter Pack
- **When to use:** trade-association meetings, agendas/minutes, benchmarking or standards activity.
- **Required starting materials:** agenda/minutes drafts, participant list, talking points, benchmarking data proposals.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** trade-association-meeting-review -> information-sharing-clean-team-review -> antitrust-compliance-policy-review.
- **Expected outputs:** red-flag topic list, agenda edits for attorney review, information-sharing safeguards, protocol/policy improvements.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before meeting attendance or sharing statements/data.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no statement that attendance is safe.
- **Handoff notes:** meeting review flags topics; flags determine clean-team boundaries; both feed policy and training controls.
