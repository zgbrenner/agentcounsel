# Antitrust / Competition Matter Packs

All packs produce **draft-only work product for attorney review**. Outputs are not legality conclusions, merger-clearance advice, HSR/reportability conclusions, or approval of competitor conduct.

**Quality check.** Before a compliance memo or filing-adjacent draft from any
pack below reaches the final attorney-review checkpoint, run it through
`review-panels/regulatory-risk-panel.md` for a compliance, source, and
business-risk review.

## 1. Competitor Collaboration Matter Pack
- **When to use:** competitor collaborations, JVs, benchmarking, standards, joint purchasing/marketing/R&D, or settlement-adjacent collaboration planning.
- **Required starting materials:** jurisdiction, industry/market context, party roles, collaboration docs, information-sharing proposals, governance terms, timeline/urgency.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** antitrust-risk-intake -> competitor-collaboration-review -> information-sharing-clean-team-review -> antitrust-compliance-policy-review.
- **Expected outputs:** intake summary, collaboration risk matrix, information-sharing controls/gaps, policy/training updates.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before competitor communications, data exchange, pricing decisions, and policy adoption.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** do not use for final legality determinations.
- **Handoff notes:** intake facts seed collaboration matrix; matrix outputs define clean-team controls; control gaps feed policy updates.

## 2. Pricing / Algorithmic Pricing Matter Pack
- **When to use:** algorithmic pricing, common-vendor tools, competitor-adjacent datasets, or automated recommendation workflows.
- **Required starting materials:** tool architecture, data dictionaries, vendor contracts, governance/audit controls, override logs, retention settings.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** antitrust-risk-intake -> pricing-algorithm-risk-triage -> information-sharing-clean-team-review -> antitrust-compliance-policy-review.
- **Expected outputs:** risk triage matrix, vendor diligence list, clean-team safeguards, policy/training escalation items.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before deployment, parameter changes, data-source expansion, and pricing governance sign-off.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** do not use to declare pricing conduct legal or safe.
- **Handoff notes:** triage identifies sensitive data flows; clean-team review translates flows into controls; policy review operationalizes controls.

## 3. M&A Antitrust Matter Pack
- **When to use:** transactions with overlap/vertical/data/labor concerns, pre-closing coordination risk, or integration planning.
- **Required starting materials:** transaction summary, overlap facts, ownership/governance rights, diligence docs, clean-team protocol drafts, integration roadmap.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** merger-antitrust-issue-spotter -> information-sharing-clean-team-review -> gun-jumping-clean-team-checklist -> antitrust-compliance-policy-review.
- **Expected outputs:** issue list, filing/fact questions for counsel, sharing-control gaps, pre-closing guardrails, policy updates.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before filing decisions, pre-closing actions, closing, and Day-1 integration actions.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** do not use for reportability/threshold/clearance conclusions.
- **Handoff notes:** issue-spotter creates diligence questions; clean-team review sets boundaries; gun-jumping checklist aligns pre-close behavior.

## 4. Distribution / Channel Strategy Matter Pack
- **When to use:** channel programs with MAP, territory/customer limits, marketplace restrictions, or exclusivity/MFN/loyalty terms.
- **Required starting materials:** drafts/policies, channel map, pricing terms, affected products/customers, business rationale, termination provisions.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** distribution-restraints-review -> exclusivity-mfn-pricing-review -> antitrust-risk-intake.
- **Expected outputs:** restraints table, clause-risk matrix, negotiation points, consolidated risk-intake and open-fact list.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before issuing dealer policies, negotiating clauses, and implementing restrictions.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** do not use for enforceability or legality conclusions.
- **Handoff notes:** restraints review maps vertical limits; clause review deepens exposure themes; intake consolidates escalations.

## 5. Trade Association Matter Pack
- **When to use:** agenda/minutes/talking-point review for trade-association meetings and benchmarking/standards working groups.
- **Required starting materials:** agenda drafts, minutes, participant list, talking points, benchmarking proposals, follow-up communications.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** trade-association-meeting-review -> information-sharing-clean-team-review -> antitrust-compliance-policy-review.
- **Expected outputs:** agenda/minutes issue list, red-flag topics, editing suggestions for attorney review, meeting protocols and policy updates.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before attendance, statements, data-sharing, and post-meeting follow-up.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** do not use to declare attendance or discussion topics safe.
- **Handoff notes:** meeting review identifies sensitive topics; clean-team review constrains data sharing; policy review hardens meeting protocol.

## 6. Antitrust Compliance Program Matter Pack
- **When to use:** enterprise compliance baseline or remediation across competitor contacts, pricing, distribution, M&A, and association activities.
- **Required starting materials:** existing policy set, escalation paths, training logs, certifications, incident reports, business process maps.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** antitrust-risk-intake -> antitrust-compliance-policy-review -> trade-association-meeting-review -> information-sharing-clean-team-review -> pricing-algorithm-risk-triage.
- **Expected outputs:** enterprise risk-intake, policy gap matrix, meeting protocol edits, data-sharing controls, algorithmic-pricing safeguards.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** before rolling out policies, training, certifications, audits, and enforcement actions.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** do not use to certify legal sufficiency or compliance outcomes.
- **Handoff notes:** intake scopes risk domains; policy review sets framework; meeting/clean-team/pricing skills provide operational controls.
