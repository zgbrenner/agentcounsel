# Real Estate Matter Packs

Workflow bundles for the Real Estate practice area — recommended sequences of
AgentCounsel skills for common, multi-step real estate matters. See
`matter-packs/README.md` for what a matter pack is and how to use one.

These packs are process guidance, not legal advice. Every skill they sequence
produces draft work product that a qualified, licensed attorney must review.
Before starting, read the `core/` operating rules and `practice-profiles/real-estate.md`,
and confirm the jurisdiction, property type, party role, transaction posture,
and document set for the matter. None of these skills computes a deadline,
states jurisdiction-specific law, or reaches a title, zoning, or other legal
conclusion — those remain attorney work.

**Quality check.** Before a drafted or marked-up agreement from any pack below
reaches the final attorney-review checkpoint, run it through
`review-panels/contract-review-panel.md` for a multi-lens issue, position, and
risk review.

## 1. Commercial Lease Review Matter Pack

**When to use.** Reviewing a commercial lease that is in place or under
negotiation — for a leasing decision, a lease-up review, or as part of a larger
transaction — where amendments, estoppels, or an SNDA may be involved.

**Required starting materials.** The full lease; all amendments, side letters,
and assignments; any estoppel certificates or SNDAs; the party role the review
is for; the property type; and the jurisdiction.

**Recommended skill sequence.**

| Step | Skill | Produces | Feeds the next step |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `skills/real-estate/lease-abstract/SKILL.md` | A source-cited abstract of the base lease | The abstracted terms |
| 2 | `skills/real-estate/lease-amendment-reconciliation/SKILL.md` | The current controlling terms across the lease and its amendments | The controlling terms |
| 3 | `skills/real-estate/commercial-lease-review/SKILL.md` | A perspective-based risk matrix and prioritized issue list | The issue list |
| 4 | `skills/real-estate/estoppel-snda-review/SKILL.md` | An estoppel/SNDA review and a lease-versus-estoppel discrepancy table | Any discrepancies |
| 5 | `skills/real-estate/closing-deliverables-tracker/SKILL.md` | A deliverables tracker, if the lease ties to a closing | — |

**Expected outputs.** A source-cited lease abstract; a controlling-term
reconciliation; a risk matrix and prioritized issue list; an estoppel/SNDA
discrepancy review; and, where relevant, a closing-deliverables tracker.

**Attorney verification checkpoints.**
- After step 2 — confirm the controlling terms before the substantive review relies on them.
- After step 3 — confirm the issue priorities and the suggested redline directions.
- Before any estoppel or SNDA is signed or returned (after step 4).
- Before closing, signing, or relying on the lease.

**Do not use when.** The document is a purchase and sale agreement (use Matter
Pack 2); the matter is purely a title, survey, or use-restriction question (use
Matter Pack 4); or the document is not a lease.

## 2. Real Estate Acquisition Matter Pack

**When to use.** Acquiring (or disposing of) real property under a purchase and
sale agreement, from contract review through diligence to closing preparation.

**Required starting materials.** The purchase and sale agreement; the party
role (buyer, seller, or lender); the property type; the jurisdiction; and the
diligence document set as it is assembled (title commitment, survey, leases,
service contracts, environmental reports, and similar).

**Recommended skill sequence.**

| Step | Skill | Produces | Feeds the next step |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `skills/real-estate/psa-review/SKILL.md` | A risk matrix and issue list on the PSA, and a key-dates table | The diligence obligations and key dates |
| 2 | `skills/real-estate/real-estate-diligence-checklist/SKILL.md` | A tailored diligence checklist and a missing-document request list | The diligence scope |
| 3 | `skills/real-estate/title-survey-objection-tracker/SKILL.md` | A title and survey objection tracker | Title and survey issues |
| 4 | `skills/real-estate/zoning-use-restriction-issue-spotter/SKILL.md` | Zoning and use-restriction issues and questions for local counsel | Use and zoning issues |
| 5 | `skills/real-estate/closing-deliverables-tracker/SKILL.md` | A closing-deliverables tracker | — |

**Expected outputs.** A PSA risk review with a key-dates table; a tailored
diligence checklist and document-request list; a title and survey objection
tracker; a zoning and use-restriction issue list with questions for local
counsel; and a closing-deliverables tracker.

**Attorney verification checkpoints.**
- After step 1 — confirm the PSA issues and, critically, every diligence and
  closing date (the skill flags dates; it never computes them).
- After step 3 — confirm title and survey objections before any objection is sent.
- After step 4 — route every zoning and use question to local counsel or a
  zoning consultant; the skill reaches no conclusion on permitted use.
- Before closing, signing, recording, or sending any notice.

**Do not use when.** The matter is a lease review with no acquisition (use
Matter Pack 1); or the transaction is not a real property purchase or sale.

## 3. Lease Amendment / Portfolio Review Matter Pack

**When to use.** Reviewing a lease — or a portfolio of leases — with a
significant amendment history, for example a pre-financing or pre-sale lease
audit, or a portfolio review where controlling terms must be established
before any assessment.

**Required starting materials.** For each lease: the base lease; every
amendment, side letter, and assignment; any estoppels; the party role; and the
property type. Note any amendment referenced but not located.

**Recommended skill sequence.**

| Step | Skill | Produces | Feeds the next step |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `skills/real-estate/lease-abstract/SKILL.md` | A source-cited abstract of each base lease | The abstracted terms |
| 2 | `skills/real-estate/lease-amendment-reconciliation/SKILL.md` | The current controlling terms, conflicts, and missing amendments | The controlling terms |
| 3 | `skills/real-estate/estoppel-snda-review/SKILL.md` | An estoppel review and lease-versus-estoppel discrepancies | Any discrepancies |
| 4 | `skills/real-estate/commercial-lease-review/SKILL.md` | A risk matrix and prioritized issue list per lease | — |

**Expected outputs.** Per lease: a source-cited abstract; a controlling-term
reconciliation with conflicts and missing-amendment flags; an estoppel
discrepancy review; and a risk matrix and issue list.

**Attorney verification checkpoints.**
- After step 2 — confirm controlling terms and resolve every conflict and
  missing-amendment flag before relying on the reconciliation.
- After step 3 — confirm estoppel discrepancies.
- Before the review is relied upon for financing, sale, or negotiation.

**Do not use when.** A single lease with no amendment history (Matter Pack 1 is
lighter); or the matter is an acquisition under a PSA (use Matter Pack 2).

## 4. Title / Survey / Use Restriction Matter Pack

**When to use.** A focused title, survey, and land-use review — for example a
development feasibility assessment, a pre-acquisition title and survey
objection round, or a use-change evaluation — where the deal documents are not
the primary focus.

**Required starting materials.** The title commitment or report and its
exception documents; the survey; the intended use and operations; any recorded
restrictions, CC&Rs, and permits; the party role; the property type; and the
jurisdiction.

**Recommended skill sequence.**

| Step | Skill | Produces | Feeds the next step |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `skills/real-estate/title-survey-objection-tracker/SKILL.md` | A title and survey objection tracker | Title, survey, and recorded-restriction issues |
| 2 | `skills/real-estate/zoning-use-restriction-issue-spotter/SKILL.md` | Zoning and use-restriction issues and questions for local counsel | Use, zoning, and restriction issues |
| 3 | `skills/real-estate/real-estate-diligence-checklist/SKILL.md` | A tailored diligence checklist capturing remaining open items | — |

**Expected outputs.** A title and survey objection tracker; a zoning and
use-restriction issue list with questions for local counsel or a zoning
consultant; and a diligence checklist of remaining open items and documents to
request.

**Attorney verification checkpoints.**
- After step 1 — the tracker never states that title is marketable, insurable,
  or clear; the attorney and the title company make that determination.
- After step 2 — route every zoning and permitted-use question to local
  counsel; the skill states no conclusion on whether a use is allowed.
- Before any objection, application, or notice is sent.

**Do not use when.** The matter needs a full contract or lease review (use
Matter Pack 1 or 2); or controlling authority on a use question is required —
that is local counsel's work, not a skill output.
