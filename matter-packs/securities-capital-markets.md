# Securities / Capital Markets Matter Packs

## Private Placement Matter Pack
- **When to use:** private offering planning and execution.
- **Required starting materials:** issuer profile, offering terms, investor facts, draft documents.
- **Recommended skill sequence:**
  1. securities-exemption-issue-spotter
  2. private-placement-checklist
  3. investor-rights-agreement-review
  4. form-d-blue-sky-tracker
  5. capital-markets-closing-checklist
- **Expected outputs:** pathway issue list, process checklist, agreement issue matrix, filing tracker, closing tracker.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** exemption conclusions, filing obligations, deadlines, closing sign-off.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no final legal advice, no deadline calculations, no investment advice.
- **Handoff notes:** feed unresolved exemption/fact gaps into checklist and filing tracker.

## Offering Document Review Matter Pack
- **When to use:** disclosure review for PPM/prospectus/registration materials.
- **Required starting materials:** current and prior drafts, related filings, support docs.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** offering-document-disclosure-review -> risk-factor-review -> sec-filing-consistency-check -> comfort-backup-request-tracker -> capital-markets-closing-checklist
- **Expected outputs:** issue list, risk inventory, inconsistency log, backup tracker, closing deliverables tracker.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** disclosure adequacy, unsupported claims, filing/closing approvals.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no compliance certification, no final filing approval.
- **Handoff notes:** inconsistencies and unsupported statements flow into comfort backup and closing workstreams.

## Public Company Reporting Matter Pack
- **When to use:** reporting-process intake and controls setup.
- **Required starting materials:** filer profile, governance calendar, filing drafts/policies.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** public-company-reporting-calendar-intake -> sec-filing-consistency-check -> risk-factor-review -> insider-trading-policy-review -> section-16-beneficial-ownership-triage
- **Expected outputs:** workflow checklist, consistency tracker, risk-factor issues, policy gap list, ownership triage facts table.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** reporting obligations, deadlines, insider/beneficial-ownership conclusions.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no deadline computations, no final legal conclusions.
- **Handoff notes:** intake gaps should pre-populate policy and ownership triage questions.

## Insider / Beneficial Ownership Matter Pack
- **When to use:** insider-trading governance and ownership reporting triage.
- **Required starting materials:** insider policy docs, cap table/ownership data, role data.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** insider-trading-policy-review -> section-16-beneficial-ownership-triage -> public-company-reporting-calendar-intake
- **Expected outputs:** policy issue list, filing-regime triage matrix, reporting workflow intake.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** trade permissibility, Section 16/13D-13G status, filing deadlines.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no trade approvals, no final filing status calls.
- **Handoff notes:** policy ambiguities and ownership gaps should inform calendar controls.

## Capital Markets Closing Matter Pack
- **When to use:** offering closing management for private or public transactions.
- **Required starting materials:** transaction docs, disclosure drafts, support data, role assignments.
- **Recommended skill sequence:** offering-document-disclosure-review -> comfort-backup-request-tracker -> capital-markets-closing-checklist (+ form-d-blue-sky-tracker if private offering) (+ sec-filing-consistency-check if public filing docs provided)
- **Expected outputs:** disclosure issues, backup tracker, closing deliverables tracker, conditional filing/consistency trackers.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** signing/closing approval, filing readiness, factual support sufficiency.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no closing sign-off by model.
- **Handoff notes:** unresolved support requests become closing conditions/open items.
