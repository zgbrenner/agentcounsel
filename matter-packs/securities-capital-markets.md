# Securities / Capital Markets Matter Packs

All packs produce **draft work product for attorney review only**. Outputs are not filing approval, exemption conclusions, trading advice, investment advice, or permission to sign/close.

**Quality check.** Before a filing or disclosure draft from any pack below
reaches the final attorney-review checkpoint, run it through
`review-panels/regulatory-risk-panel.md` for a compliance, source, and
business-risk review.

## Private Placement Matter Pack
- **When to use:** Private financing from intake through closing coordination.
- **Required starting materials:** issuer profile; jurisdiction/governing law (or unknown); issuer public/private status; transaction type/stage; security type; investor type and residence; draft offering docs; investor-rights drafts; role assignments.
- **Recommended skill sequence:**
  1. securities-exemption-issue-spotter
  2. private-placement-checklist
  3. investor-rights-agreement-review
  4. form-d-blue-sky-tracker
  5. capital-markets-closing-checklist
- **Expected outputs:** pathway-to-evaluate matrix; workstream checklist; key terms and risk matrix; filing tracker; closing deliverable tracker.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** exemption theory, blue-sky obligations, placement-agent/bad-actor implications, filing/signing/closing readiness.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** not for final exemption opinions, filing deadlines, or investor communications without counsel sign-off.
- **Handoff notes:** unresolved pathway facts from step 1 become open items in checklist/tracker steps.

## Offering Document Review Matter Pack
- **When to use:** Multi-draft disclosure and consistency quality control for offering materials.
- **Required starting materials:** current and prior drafts (PPM/prospectus/registration statement supplements), supporting schedules, management comments, financial tables.
- **Recommended skill sequence:**
  1. offering-document-disclosure-review
  2. risk-factor-review
  3. sec-filing-consistency-check
  4. comfort-backup-request-tracker
  5. capital-markets-closing-checklist
- **Expected outputs:** disclosure issue list; risk inventory; inconsistency tracker; support-request tracker; deliverables tracker.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** adequacy/materiality judgments, conflict resolution, disclosure and filing readiness.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** not SEC compliance certification and not filing approval.
- **Handoff notes:** items flagged as unsupported/conflicting feed directly into comfort and closing trackers.

## Public Company Reporting Matter Pack
- **When to use:** Reporting process intake and recurring disclosure-control workflows.
- **Required starting materials:** filer profile, fiscal year-end, reporting document set, committee calendar, insider-policy materials, ownership data.
- **Recommended skill sequence:**
  1. public-company-reporting-calendar-intake
  2. sec-filing-consistency-check
  3. risk-factor-review
  4. insider-trading-policy-review
  5. section-16-beneficial-ownership-triage
- **Expected outputs:** workflow checklist; inconsistency tracker; risk-factor gaps; policy-gap matrix; facts-to-verify table.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** reporting obligations, deadlines, Section 16 / 13D-13G conclusions, policy updates.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** not for deadline computation or trading determinations.
- **Handoff notes:** missing intake facts should propagate into every downstream output as explicit unknowns.

## Insider / Beneficial Ownership Matter Pack
- **When to use:** Coordinated review of insider-policy procedures and ownership-reporting fact patterns.
- **Required starting materials:** insider-trading policy, role roster, ownership/cap-table snapshots, derivative/instrument summaries, escalation process docs.
- **Recommended skill sequence:**
  1. insider-trading-policy-review
  2. section-16-beneficial-ownership-triage
  3. public-company-reporting-calendar-intake
- **Expected outputs:** procedure-gap table; filing-regime candidates to evaluate; reporting-workflow ownership matrix.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** trade-permissibility decisions, insider/group status, filing obligations/deadlines.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** no buy/sell/hold recommendations or individual trading approvals.
- **Handoff notes:** policy ambiguities and ownership gaps should become calendar intake controls.

## Capital Markets Closing Matter Pack
- **When to use:** Closing coordination for private or public capital markets transactions.
- **Required starting materials:** near-final transaction documents, disclosure drafts, comfort/back-up support tracker, signer list, funds-flow draft.
- **Recommended skill sequence:**
  1. offering-document-disclosure-review
  2. comfort-backup-request-tracker
  3. capital-markets-closing-checklist
  4. form-d-blue-sky-tracker *(if private offering)*
  5. sec-filing-consistency-check *(if public filing documents are provided)*
- **Expected outputs:** closing-ready issue list, numbered support tracker, dependency-based closing tracker, conditional filing/consistency trackers.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** signing authority, required deliverables, filing submissions, post-closing actions.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** not a legal opinion on required parties/filings.
- **Handoff notes:** any unresolved source gaps remain open conditions to closing until counsel resolves.

## Public Offering / Registration Statement Matter Pack
- **When to use:** Registration statement and offering-document review cycles.
- **Required starting materials:** draft registration statement set, risk factors, financial extracts, support documents, prior draft redlines.
- **Recommended skill sequence:**
  1. offering-document-disclosure-review
  2. risk-factor-review
  3. sec-filing-consistency-check
  4. comfort-backup-request-tracker
  5. capital-markets-closing-checklist
- **Expected outputs:** disclosure issues; risk-factor quality review; cross-draft inconsistencies; backup requests; deliverable tracker.
- **Attorney verification checkpoints:** disclosure judgments, SEC-response strategy, offering readiness.
- **Do-not-use boundaries:** not a final registration/filing approval and not investment guidance.
- **Handoff notes:** consistency and support gaps should be tracked as gating items before filing or launch.
