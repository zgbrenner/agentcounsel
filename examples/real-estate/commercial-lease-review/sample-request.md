# Sample Request — Commercial Lease Review

*Fictional sample request to the Commercial Lease Review skill (`skills/real-estate/commercial-lease-review/SKILL.md`). Every party, property, figure, and excerpt below is invented for illustration.*

## The request

We are the **tenant**. Please review this commercial lease from our
perspective and flag the issues we should push back on before we sign.
We have not engaged outside counsel yet and want a structured starting
point.

## Inputs supplied

- **Party perspective:** Tenant — *Northwind Analytics LLC*, a software
  company taking its first dedicated office.
- **Landlord:** *Cedar Ridge Property Partners LP.*
- **Premises / property type:** Suite 410, a roughly 9,200 sq. ft. office
  suite in a five-story suburban office building ("the Building").
- **Transaction posture:** A new lease being negotiated; a seven-year term
  proposed; we have not yet countered.
- **Jurisdiction / governing law:** The lease names a U.S. state but we do
  not want to assume which controls — treat governing law as stated in the
  document and flag it.
- **Document set:** The base lease and Exhibit C (Operating Expense
  provisions). Exhibit B (Work Letter) is referenced but **was not provided**.

## Fictional lease excerpts the review should draw on

- **§ 3.1 Base Rent (p. 4):** "Base Rent shall be $28.00 per rentable
  square foot per annum for Lease Year 1, increasing by four percent (4%)
  on each anniversary of the Commencement Date."
- **§ 4.2 Operating Expenses (p. 7; Exhibit C, p. 2):** "Tenant shall pay
  Tenant's Proportionate Share of all Operating Expenses. There is no base
  year and no expense stop. Operating Expenses include capital
  expenditures and Landlord's management fee." No audit right is stated.
- **§ 6.3 Permitted Use (p. 11):** "The Premises shall be used solely for
  general office purposes and no other use whatsoever."
- **§ 9.1 Assignment and Subletting (p. 16):** "Tenant shall not assign or
  sublet without Landlord's prior written consent, which Landlord may
  grant or withhold in its sole and absolute discretion."
- **§ 12.4 Maintenance (p. 21):** "Tenant shall maintain and replace the
  HVAC system serving the Premises at Tenant's sole cost."
- **§ 15.2 Default (p. 27):** "A monetary default occurs if any sum is not
  paid when due. Tenant shall have three (3) days after written notice to
  cure a monetary default."
- **§ 19.1 Renewal Option (p. 33):** "Tenant may renew for one (1)
  additional five-year term by delivering written notice not less than
  twelve (12) months before expiration, at the then-prevailing market
  rent."
- **§ 23.0 Governing Law (p. 39):** "This Lease shall be governed by the
  laws of the State of [State name partly illegible in the provided copy]."
- *Casualty and condemnation: no provision was located in the copy
  provided.*
