# Sample Request — Title and Survey Objection Tracker

*This is a fictional sample request, invented to illustrate the Title and Survey Objection Tracker skill. No real parties, property, or documents are involved.*

## The request

We are buying an industrial parcel and need the title objections organized into a working tracker before our objection letter goes out. Please turn the title commitment and survey excerpts below into a source-cited objection list from the **buyer's** perspective. The survey is attached, but two of the recorded easement documents it references were never sent to us — please flag those rather than guess what they say.

## Inputs

- **Party role:** Buyer — Harborline Logistics Holdings, LLC.
- **Seller:** Crestmark Industrial Partners, L.P.
- **Title company:** Meridian Title Guaranty Company.
- **Parcel:** A single industrial parcel of roughly 14.2 acres, designated Tax Parcel No. 88-204-117, improved with a warehouse and a paved yard. No street address is used in this sample.
- **Documents provided:** Title Commitment No. MTG-50912 (Schedule A, Schedule B-I, Schedule B-II) and ALTA/NSPS Survey No. S-2231 (Sheets 1-3).

### Title commitment excerpts (Commitment No. MTG-50912)

- **Schedule B-I, Requirement 3** — Payoff and release of a deed of trust in favor of Riverstone Bank, N.A., recorded as Instrument 2019-44017.
- **Schedule B-II, Exception 6** — Easement for ingress and egress in favor of an adjoining owner, recorded as Instrument 2007-11820. *(Underlying recorded document NOT provided.)*
- **Schedule B-II, Exception 7** — Utility easement granted to Valley Power Cooperative, recorded as Instrument 2013-30566. *(Underlying recorded document NOT provided.)*
- **Schedule B-II, Exception 9** — Covenants, conditions, and restrictions set out in the Northgate Industrial Park Declaration, recorded as Instrument 1998-00742. *(Declaration provided.)*
- **Schedule B-II, Exception 12** — Terms of an unrecorded lease referenced in the seller's files; tenant and term not stated in the commitment.
- **Schedule B-II, Exception 14** — Taxes for the current year, a lien not yet due and payable.

### Survey excerpts (ALTA/NSPS Survey No. S-2231)

- **Survey Sheet 2, Note 4** — A 20-foot access easement plotted along the south boundary; surveyor notes it appears to correspond to a recorded instrument but the recording reference could not be located.
- **Survey Sheet 2, Note 7** — A paved drive from the adjoining parcel encroaches approximately 3 feet onto the southwest corner of the subject parcel.
- **Survey Sheet 3, Note 11** — An overhead power line crosses the northeast portion of the parcel; no easement of record was identified by the surveyor for this line.
- **Survey Sheet 3, Note 12** — A fence along the east boundary sits roughly 1.5 feet inside the platted boundary line.
