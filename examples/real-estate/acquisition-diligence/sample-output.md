## 1. Checklist Header

- **Transaction type:** Acquisition (asset purchase of the property).
- **Property type:** Multi-tenant retail center ("Maple Crossing," fictional).
- **Jurisdiction:** The state and county where the property is located — `[verify jurisdiction]`.
- **Party role:** Buyer — Lark Hollow Retail Partners, LLC.
- **Counterparty:** Seller — Cedarpoint Property Holdings, LLC.
- **Documents provided so far:** Executed PSA; preliminary one-page rent roll (undated); a prior-owner title commitment of unknown age.

## 2. Matter Framing

This checklist is built for the buyer in an acquisition of a multi-tenant retail center. It is a tailored scaffold for the diligence period, not a determination of what diligence is legally required, sufficient, or complete. Items whose scope or applicability depends on local law are marked `[ATTORNEY TO CONFIRM: local requirements]` rather than resolved here. The diligence period and any contingency dates are governed by the PSA and are flagged for verification — no date is computed in this checklist.

## 4. Missing-Document Request List

Send to the seller / counterparty:

- **Entity authority:** Seller organizational documents, good-standing evidence, authorizing resolutions.
- **Title and survey:** Existing survey; title exception documents (the buyer-side current commitment will be ordered separately).
- **Leases:** Certified and dated rent roll; all leases, amendments, and guaranties.
- **Service contracts:** Management agreement and all maintenance / vendor contracts.
- **Environmental:** Any prior environmental reports; disclosure of known conditions or remediation history.
- **Insurance:** Existing policies, certificates, and loss-run history.
- **Taxes:** Recent real estate tax bills and assessment notices; status of any appeals.
- **Financing:** Existing loan documents, if any.
- **Litigation:** Disclosure of pending or threatened litigation, liens, and judgments.
- **Permits:** Certificates of occupancy and building / operating permit records.
- **Property condition:** Any seller-held engineering or systems reports; deferred-maintenance schedule.
- **Financials:** Trailing operating statements, current budget, and CAM reconciliations.
