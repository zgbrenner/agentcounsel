## Capability and Reliance Notice

This is a draft claim-handling risk triage for a qualified, licensed attorney.
It does **not** provide legal advice, conclude that bad faith occurred or did
not occur, decide whether claim handling was reasonable or unreasonable, assess
extracontractual exposure, damages, or reserves, or apply any jurisdiction's
bad-faith standard. Every theme below is a neutral item for counsel to
evaluate. A qualified attorney must review it before any claim-handling
assessment.
