## Indemnity and Risk Allocation

Section 13 provides that the service provider indemnifies Northgate for certain
third-party claims, as written. Section 12 requires the service provider to
carry insurance and to name Northgate as an additional insured on the CGL. The
insurance requirements (§12) and the indemnity (§13) are related risk-transfer
mechanisms; how they interact, and the scope and legal effect of each, are
questions for the attorney. This review states what the clauses say and does
not determine the scope or effect of the indemnity or the additional insured
requirement.
