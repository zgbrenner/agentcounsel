# Sample Request — transaction-tax-diligence-request-list

> Fictional illustration. All parties, facts, figures, and documents below are
> invented for this example.

Please run **transaction-tax-diligence-request-list** and prepare a tax
diligence request list for our deal team.

**Matter facts**

- Transaction: proposed acquisition of Cascade Logistics Inc. by way of a stock
  purchase (fictional).
- Stage: pre-signing diligence.
- Our role: buyer-side; the user is buyer's deal counsel.
- Target profile: a multi-state logistics company; C corporation; operations in
  States A, B, and D; one foreign subsidiary mentioned in passing.
- Jurisdictions: U.S. federal and States A, B, D; foreign jurisdiction
  `not provided`.

**Workstreams in scope**

Income tax, sales/use tax, payroll/employment tax, property tax, tax returns
and audits, tax attributes (NOLs/credits), intercompany arrangements, and
foreign tax issues.

**Materials provided**

- Confidential Information Memorandum excerpt (fictional), §3 (operations).
- Preliminary diligence index (fictional), 2 pages.

**Stated facts**

- The target reports NOLs but no figures are provided.
- A sales/use tax audit in State B is mentioned as "ongoing" — no details.
- One foreign subsidiary is referenced; its jurisdiction is not stated.

Please: organize requests by workstream with priority, rationale, owner, and
source/basis; use `unknown / not provided / ambiguous` for gaps; do **not**
calculate exposure or tax liability; and flag conditional requests.
