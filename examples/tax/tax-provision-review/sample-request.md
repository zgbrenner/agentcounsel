# Sample Request — tax-provision-review-checklist

> Fictional illustration. All parties, facts, figures, and documents below are
> invented for this example.

Please run **tax-provision-review-checklist** on the tax provisions of a draft
agreement and flag the issues for our tax professional.

**Matter facts**

- Agreement: a draft Master Services Agreement (fictional) between Harborview
  Media LLC (customer) and a vendor.
- Our role: customer-side; the user is in-house counsel for Harborview.
- Transaction type: cross-border services engagement.
- Jurisdiction / governing law: the draft names State A as governing law; the
  vendor is located abroad — vendor jurisdiction `not provided`.
- Review purpose: identify tax-provision issues and negotiation points before
  signing.

**Materials provided**

- Draft MSA (fictional), with tax-related provisions at:
  - §8.1 "Taxes" — customer pays "all applicable taxes."
  - §8.2 "Withholding" — references withholding but the mechanic is
    `ambiguous`.
  - §14 "Indemnification" — general indemnity; a tax-specific indemnity is not
    located.

**Stated facts**

- The vendor will perform services partly from abroad.
- No purchase-price allocation or transfer-tax provision is expected (services
  deal).

Please: build a key tax terms table and a provision checklist with a risk
matrix from the customer's perspective; list missing provisions as `not found`
only after a full review; give negotiation points as direction only; do **not**
determine tax consequences or enforceability.
