# Sample Request — Purchase Agreement Issue List

*Fictional sample request to the Purchase Agreement Issue List skill
(`skills/m-and-a/purchase-agreement-issue-list/SKILL.md`). Every party,
company, figure, and excerpt below is invented for illustration.*

## The request

We are the **buyer** in an acquisition and have received the other side's
first draft of the stock purchase agreement. Please review it from our
perspective, flag the issues we should push back on, and give us a
structured starting point before our deal counsel marks it up.

## Inputs supplied

- **Side / perspective:** Buyer — *Brightpath Holdings Inc.*, the acquiring
  company.
- **Deal type:** Stock purchase agreement (SPA) — Buyer acquires 100% of the
  outstanding stock of the target.
- **Target / sellers:** *Larkfield Instruments Inc.* (the "Company"); its
  stock is held by three individual sellers (the "Sellers").
- **Transaction stage:** First-draft review of the Sellers' draft; no markup
  exchanged yet.
- **Jurisdiction / governing law:** The SPA names a U.S. state, but we do not
  want to assume which controls — treat governing law as stated in the
  document and flag it.
- **Document set:** The base SPA only. The disclosure schedules and the
  escrow agreement are referenced but **were not provided**.

## Fictional SPA excerpts the review should draw on

- **§ 2.1 Purchase Price (p. 6):** "The aggregate purchase price shall be
  $42,000,000 in cash, subject to adjustment under § 2.4, payable at
  Closing."
- **§ 2.4 Purchase Price Adjustment (p. 8):** "The Purchase Price shall be
  adjusted dollar-for-dollar for the difference between Closing Net Working
  Capital and the Target of $5,000,000. Sellers shall prepare the closing
  statement, which shall be final and binding unless Buyer objects within
  ten (10) days."
- **§ 2.6 Escrow (p. 10):** "At Closing, $1,000,000 of the Purchase Price
  shall be deposited into escrow and released to Sellers twelve (12) months
  after Closing."
- **§ 4 Representations and Warranties of the Sellers (p. 15):** A set of
  reps regarding the Company, each "to the Sellers' Knowledge." No reference
  to disclosure schedules appears in the body of § 4.
- **§ 6.2 Conditions to Buyer's Obligations (p. 24):** "Buyer's obligation to
  close is subject to the accuracy of the Sellers' representations in all
  material respects and the absence of any Material Adverse Effect." No
  financing condition is stated.
- **§ 8.2 Indemnification (p. 30):** "Sellers shall indemnify Buyer for
  breaches of the representations in § 4. Indemnification under this § 8.2 is
  the sole and exclusive remedy of Buyer."
- **§ 8.3 Limitations (p. 31):** "Sellers' aggregate liability shall not
  exceed the Escrow Amount. No claim may be made unless Losses exceed a
  basket of $250,000, in which case Sellers are liable only for the excess."
- **§ 8.4 Survival (p. 32):** "The representations in § 4 shall survive
  Closing for twelve (12) months."
- **§ 11.3 Governing Law (p. 41):** "This Agreement shall be governed by the
  laws of the State of [State name not legible in the copy provided]."
- *Restrictive covenants (non-compete / non-solicit by the Sellers): no
  provision was located in the copy provided.*
