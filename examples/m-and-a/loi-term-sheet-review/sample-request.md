# Sample Request — LOI and Term Sheet Review

*Fictional sample request to the LOI and Term Sheet Review skill (`skills/m-and-a/loi-term-sheet-review/SKILL.md`). Every party, company, figure, date, and excerpt below is invented for illustration.*

## The request

We are the **buyer**. Please review this term sheet for our acquisition and
give us a structured read before we countersign — what it commits us to, what
it leaves open, and what we should push back on. We have not yet engaged
deal counsel and want an organized starting point.

## Inputs supplied

- **Side:** Buyer — *Halcyon Industrial Holdings Inc.* ("Buyer"), a
  mid-market acquirer.
- **Target:** *Brightwater Components LLC* ("Target") and its members
  ("Sellers").
- **Deal type:** Stock purchase — Buyer to acquire 100% of the equity
  interests of Target.
- **Transaction stage:** Term sheet received; Buyer has not signed and is
  preparing a counter. Diligence has not started.
- **Jurisdiction / governing law:** The term sheet names a U.S. state; treat
  governing law as stated in the document and flag it rather than assume.
- **Document set:** The term sheet only. A mutual NDA is referenced but
  **was not provided**.

## Fictional term sheet excerpts the review should draw on

- **Preamble:** "This Term Sheet sets out the principal terms of the
  proposed transaction. Except for Sections 3, 4, and 9, this Term Sheet is
  non-binding and creates no obligation to proceed."
- **§ 1 (Purchase Price):** "Aggregate purchase price of $48,000,000 in
  cash, subject to a customary working-capital adjustment at closing."
- **§ 2 (Structure):** "Buyer shall purchase 100% of the issued and
  outstanding membership interests of Target from the Sellers."
- **§ 3 (Exclusivity):** "For a period of sixty (60) days from the date
  hereof, Sellers shall not solicit, negotiate, or entertain any competing
  offer. This Section is binding."
- **§ 4 (Confidentiality):** "The existence and terms of this Term Sheet are
  confidential and governed by the parties' mutual NDA. This Section is
  binding."
- **§ 5 (Diligence):** "Buyer shall have reasonable access to Target's
  books, records, and personnel during the exclusivity period."
- **§ 6 (Escrow):** "10% of the purchase price shall be held in escrow for
  18 months to secure indemnification obligations."
- **§ 7 (Closing Conditions):** "Closing is conditioned on completion of
  diligence satisfactory to Buyer and execution of a definitive agreement."
- **§ 8 (Governing Law):** "This Term Sheet is governed by the laws of the
  State of [State name not legible in the copy provided]."
- **§ 9 (Expenses):** "Each party bears its own expenses. This Section is
  binding."
- *The term sheet states a target signing date of "on or about August 15"
  and is silent on financing, earnout, and employee retention.*
