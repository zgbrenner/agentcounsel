# Sample Request — Reps and Warranties Disclosure Schedule Review

*Fictional sample request to the Reps and Warranties Disclosure Schedule
Review skill (`skills/m-and-a/reps-warranties-disclosure-schedule-review/SKILL.md`).
Every party, company, figure, and excerpt below is invented for illustration.*

## The request

We are the **buyer** in a stock purchase and have received the seller's
disclosure schedules. Please compare the representations and warranties
article against the schedules, map each rep to its schedule, and tell us
what is still open before our deal counsel signs off.

## Inputs supplied

- **Side / perspective:** Buyer — *Northcape Capital Partners LLC*, the
  acquiring entity.
- **Deal type:** Stock purchase — Buyer acquires 100% of the outstanding
  stock of the target.
- **Target / seller:** *Verdano Beverage Co.* (the "Company"); its stock is
  held by *Hollmin Family Holdings LLC* (the "Seller").
- **Transaction stage:** Pre-signing review of the Seller's first delivery of
  the disclosure schedules.
- **Jurisdiction / governing law:** The SPA names a U.S. state in § 12.4, but
  we do not want to assume which controls — treat governing law as stated and
  flag it.
- **Document set:** The SPA representations article (Article 4) and the
  disclosure schedules bundle, marked "Draft — v1." The escrow agreement is
  referenced but was not provided.
- **Known diligence fact:** Our quality-of-earnings team identified a supply
  agreement with *Cobalt Glassworks Inc.* that appears material; we want to
  know whether it shows up in the schedules.

## Fictional representations article excerpts (Article 4)

- **§ 4.5 (Financial Statements):** "The Financial Statements fairly present
  the financial condition of the Company in all material respects."
  *Stated as unqualified — no schedule reference in the text.*
- **§ 4.7 (Material Contracts):** "Except as set forth on Schedule 4.7, the
  Company is not a party to any Material Contract."
- **§ 4.9 (Litigation):** "Except as set forth on Schedule 4.9, there is no
  pending or threatened litigation against the Company."
- **§ 4.11 (Intellectual Property):** "Except as set forth on Schedule 4.11,
  the Company owns or licenses all Intellectual Property used in its business."
- **§ 4.14 (Employee Benefit Plans):** "Schedule 4.14 lists each Benefit Plan
  of the Company."
- **§ 4.16 (Environmental Matters):** "Except as set forth on Schedule 4.16,
  the Company is in compliance with Environmental Laws."

## Fictional disclosure schedule excerpts (Draft — v1)

- **Schedule 4.7 (Material Contracts):** Lists "Distribution Agreement with
  Pell & Crowe Distributors (dated as of March 2, 2022)" and "Equipment Lease
  with Tarrow Leasing (dated as of July 2021)." Closes with the entry
  "and various other agreements entered into in the ordinary course."
- **Schedule 4.9 (Litigation):** "[to be provided]"
- **Schedule 4.11 (Intellectual Property):** Lists registered marks; refers
  to "Company IP" rather than the defined term "Intellectual Property."
- **Schedule 4.14 (Employee Benefit Plans):** Lists a 401(k) plan and a
  health plan; the schedule is labeled "Schedule 4.13."
- *No Schedule 4.16 appears anywhere in the bundle.*
- **Schedule 4.20 (Insurance):** Lists three insurance policies; Article 4
  has no § 4.20 in the excerpts provided.
