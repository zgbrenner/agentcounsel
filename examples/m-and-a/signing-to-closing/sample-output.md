## 4. Scope and Basis Note

This tracker was derived from the **signed SPA excerpts** (§§ 2.3, 2.4, 6.1,
6.2, 7.1, 9.2) and a **list of ancillary documents** provided with the
request. Where a deliverable is drawn from the SPA, the row cites the section;
items marked "General scaffold" (signature packets, wire verification, escrow
account opening, lender funding mechanics) are typical closing items the
excerpts do not address and must be confirmed as required, or removed, by
counsel and the deal team. Schedule 6.1 and the executed ancillary drafts were
not provided; deliverables touching them are provisional. The full SPA,
including all closing-conditions and closing-deliverables provisions, must be
reconciled against this tracker. This tracker is a project-management aid, not
a determination that the deal is ready to close.
