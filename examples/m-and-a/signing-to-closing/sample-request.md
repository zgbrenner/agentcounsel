# Sample Request — M&A Closing Deliverables Tracker

*Fictional sample request to the M&A Closing Deliverables Tracker skill
(`skills/m-and-a/closing-deliverables-tracker/SKILL.md`). Every party,
company, figure, and excerpt below is invented for illustration.*

## The request

We have **signed** the stock purchase agreement and are now working toward
**closing**. Please build us a closing-deliverables tracker so our deal team
can drive the sign-to-close period: list what each side owes at Closing, who
is responsible, the status, the dependencies, and any signature or notary
needs. We are the buyer. This is for project management — our deal counsel
will reconcile it against the agreement.

## Inputs supplied

- **Side / perspective:** Buyer — *Nordvale Acquisition Corp.*
- **Deal type:** Stock purchase — Buyer acquires 100% of the stock of the
  target.
- **Parties:**
  - **Buyer:** *Nordvale Acquisition Corp.*
  - **Seller:** *Calloway Family Holdings LLC* (sole stockholder of the
    target, *Brightmere Logistics Inc.*, the "Company").
  - **Escrow agent:** *Tarnhill Trust Company.*
  - **Lender:** *Hartwell Capital Partners* (Buyer's acquisition financing
    source).
- **Transaction stage:** Signing-to-closing. The purchase agreement is signed;
  the deal team is assembling closing deliverables.
- **Jurisdiction / governing law:** The agreement names a U.S. state; treat
  governing law as stated in the document and flag it.
- **Document set:** The signed Stock Purchase Agreement (the "SPA") and a list
  of ancillary documents. Disclosure schedules and the escrow agreement are
  referenced but **were not provided**.

## Fictional SPA excerpts the tracker should draw on

- **§ 2.3 Closing Deliverables (p. 9):** "At Closing, Seller shall deliver to
  Buyer: (a) stock certificates and stock powers; (b) payoff letters and
  evidence of lien releases for all Company indebtedness; (c) the Escrow
  Agreement, duly executed; (d) a non-foreign-status certificate from Seller;
  and (e) resignations of the Company's directors and officers."
- **§ 2.4 Buyer Deliverables (p. 10):** "At Closing, Buyer shall deliver the
  Closing Cash Payment by wire transfer and the Escrow Amount to the Escrow
  Agent, in each case per the Funds Flow Memorandum."
- **§ 6.1 Conditions to Buyer's Obligations (p. 22):** "Buyer's obligation to
  close is subject to delivery of (a) a Secretary's certificate of the Company
  attaching organizational documents and authorizing resolutions; (b) an
  officer's bring-down certificate; (c) good-standing certificates for the
  Company; and (d) all Required Consents listed on Schedule 6.1."
- **§ 6.2 Conditions to Seller's Obligations (p. 24):** "Seller's obligation to
  close is subject to delivery of Buyer's officer's and Secretary's
  certificates and authorizing resolutions of Buyer's board."
- **§ 7.1 Escrow (p. 27):** "$1,200,000 of the Purchase Price shall be
  deposited with the Escrow Agent at Closing under the Escrow Agreement."
- **§ 9.2 Ancillary Agreements (p. 33):** "At Closing the parties shall
  execute the Transition Services Agreement, an Intellectual Property
  Assignment, and an Employment Agreement with the Company's CEO."
- *List of ancillary documents provided with the request:* Escrow Agreement,
  Transition Services Agreement, IP Assignment, CEO Employment Agreement, and
  a Funds Flow Memorandum (referenced; drafts not attached).
- **Target closing date the user supplied:** "We are aiming to close on
  *September 18, 2026*."
