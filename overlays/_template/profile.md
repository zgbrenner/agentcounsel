A sector configuration profile, in the same style as the files under
`practice-profiles/`. It records how a team's work in **[sector]** differs from
the general case.

This profile is **not** legal work product and **not** legal advice. It is
internal configuration. Keep it generic — record positions, thresholds, and
process rules, never facts about a specific matter or client. It must be
reviewed and approved by a supervising attorney before it is loaded alongside
any skill.
