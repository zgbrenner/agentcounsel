# NDA Review — Routing to M&A Specialist

> **Draft for attorney review. Not legal advice.** This is draft attorney work product. The skill does not provide legal advice and is not a substitute for the supervising attorney's judgment.

## Routing Determination — Out of Scope

The user has identified the NDA as the confidentiality agreement for a **potential acquisition** of the counterparty. The skill's *Do Not Use When* gate routes the matter to M&A specialist counsel rather than treating the document as a routine commercial NDA. No final GREEN, YELLOW, or RED triage rating is issued.

## Why This Skill Does Not Apply

- M&A confidentiality terms carry deal-specific risk (standstills, exclusivity, sharing among advisors, club provisions, clean-room handling) that is outside the triage scope of the NDA Review skill.
- Standstill and exclusivity terms are not confidentiality terms; they govern deal conduct and require specialist deal-counsel review.

## Observations Flagged for Specialist Counsel

- **Standstill provision.** Out-of-scope for this skill; route to M&A counsel for evaluation against the proposed deal structure and timing.
- **Exclusivity clause.** Out-of-scope; route to M&A counsel. Exclusivity commitments at the NDA stage have leverage implications for the broader transaction.
- **Acquisition context.** The receiving party's confidentiality obligations interact with diligence-data handling, regulatory clean teams, and `[CONFIRM: any antitrust review applicable to the transaction]`.

## What This Skill Will Not Do

- Will not authorize signature.
- Will not characterize the NDA as low risk or recommend signature on an expedited basis.
- Will not assign a final triage rating; the document is out of this skill's scope.
