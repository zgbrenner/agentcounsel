- Asymmetric return-and-destruction obligations leave the disclosing party able to retain client materials indefinitely.

   - *Suggested Redline Direction:* Remove or narrow scope and shorten duration.
2. **Confidentiality Term (Section 3).** Issue: perpetual with no exclusions.
   - *Preferred Position:* 3-year term; add standard exclusions.
   - *Fallback Position:* 5-year term; add standard exclusions.
   - *Suggested Redline Direction:* Add a defined term and add `[standard exclusions list]` to the exclusion clause.
3. **Return and Destruction (Section 4).** Issue: one-sided.
   - *Preferred Position:* Mutual return-and-destruction with certification on request.
   - *Fallback Position:* Mutual return; certification on written request only.
   - *Suggested Redline Direction:* Make obligations mutual.
